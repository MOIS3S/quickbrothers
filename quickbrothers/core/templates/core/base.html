About us

The Company

We are a service company that is dedicated to the installation of wall protectors; Our priority is to meet all the demands of our customers and we believe that the small details make the difference for the successful result.

Our Mission

Our Mission is to give an excellent service to our clients, satisfying their demands, and exceeding their expectations in the project. For us, there is no such large or small work, all projects deserve dedication and responsibility.

Our Vision

Our Vision is to be the number “One” company in installation of wall protectors in the state of Florida, giving solutions and advances in the installation issue.

How We Work

Services

Wall Protection Covering+Panels

  • High Impact Wall Covering
  • Design Wall Covering
  • Stainless Steel Wall Covering
  • Walls Panels

Wall Protection Crash rail and Handrail.

  • Crash Rail: protect your walls with the installation of Crash Rails in different materials such as wood, metal and rubber.
  • Handrails: can aid in any facility´s desire to ease movement while maintaining a cohesive design. Available in wood, metal and rubber.

Wall protection Corner Guards and Frames Door.

  • Corner Guards: covers all angles of interior walls, are available in designs and material options such as wood, metal, polycarbonate and rubber.
  • Frames Door: the door protection to reduce damage and maintenance costs. We offer kick/push plates, full door cladding, door edge protectors, door knob protector, latch protectors, and door frame protection.
